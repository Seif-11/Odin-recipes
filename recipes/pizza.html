Pizza

Descriptions

This is how to make pizza

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Salami
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Spreed the yeast into an circle steel pan
  2. Spreed the tomato sauce on the yeast
  3. Spreed cheese on it
  4. Put the salami all over it
  5. Put all in oven for 30 min
  6. Get it out and cut it in triangles
  7. It's ready to eat
